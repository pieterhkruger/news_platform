Journalist Journalist Terms

Applies to all accounts registered with the Journalist role, in addition to the Reader Terms above.

  • You agree to maintain unbiased, factual reporting in every article you submit to the platform. Speculation must be clearly labelled as such.
  • You agree not to publish articles that constitute, contain, or are materially influenced by advertising, sponsored content, or any other promotional material.
  • You agree not to use your journalist status on The Daily Indaba for personal financial gain, reputational advancement unrelated to your published work, or any form of undisclosed conflict of interest.

  • Where you recognise, or are made aware of, potential bias in an article you have written, you must include a clear disclaimer within the article body before submission. Failure to do so may result in the article being rejected or removed.

Editor Editor Terms

Applies to all accounts registered with the Editor role, in addition to the Reader Terms above.

  • You agree to exercise editorial judgement fairly and impartially, without favouring any political party, ideological movement, commercial interest, or personal relationship.
  • You agree not to allow personal agenda or political bias to influence which articles you approve, reject, or edit.
  • Your personal data will be processed in accordance with POPIA and The Daily Indaba's Privacy Policy.
  • Where you recognise potential bias in an article you are reviewing, you must either require the journalist to add a disclaimer before approval, or add a clearly marked editorial disclaimer yourself. Approving demonstrably biased content without a disclaimer constitutes a breach of these terms.

Publisher Publisher Terms

  • Your organisation and account data will be processed in accordance with POPIA and The Daily Indaba's Privacy Policy.
  • You agree not to use the publisher role to misrepresent editorial ownership, suppress required disclaimers, or interfere improperly with article approval decisions assigned to affiliated editors.
