Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

The Daily Indaba collects only the information required to operate a subscription-based news platform. We do not sell personal data, and we do not share reader activity with advertisers because the platform carries no advertising.

Version March 2026 (v1). This policy should be read together with our Terms and Conditions.

What We Collect

  • Account information such as username, email address, role, and encrypted password.
  • Profile information you choose to add, including display name, biography, and profile picture.
  • Subscription and billing preferences, including journalist, publisher, and all-articles plans selected on the platform.
  • Platform activity required to deliver the service, such as articles authored, newsletters created, comments posted, and subscription actions.

How We Use It

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To provide access to subscribed content and manage subscription status.
  • To operate editorial, commenting, subscription, and publisher workflows on the platform.
  • To send essential service emails such as password resets, account notices, and subscription confirmations.

How We Protect and Share Data

We process personal information in line with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

  • We do not sell personal information to advertisers, brokers, or unrelated third parties.
  • We limit data access to the platform functions and roles that require it.
  • We may retain records needed for legal compliance, security, and audit purposes.
  • Where disclosure is required by law, court order, or a legitimate enforcement request, we may disclose the minimum information necessary.

Your Rights

  • You may review and update your account details through your profile.
  • You may request account deletion, subject to any records we must retain for legal or operational reasons.
  • You may contact us to ask what personal information we hold about you or to raise a privacy concern.

Privacy Contact

For privacy-related questions, access requests, or complaints, contact us at privacy@dailyindaba.co.za.

General editorial and corrections contacts remain available on the Contact page.
